Livingroom Fireplace

  • Ensure the chimney vent is open before starting a fire.
  • Firewood is not provided; please bring your own or purchase locally.
  • Never leave the fire unattended and ensure it is completely out before leaving or going to bed.
  • The fire extinguisher is located underneath the kitchen sink, if needed.

Outside Firepit

  • We provide a Solo Stove for your use, which ensures a smokeless and efficient fire.
  • Firewood must be purchased or brought by guests, as it is not provided.
  • To use the Solo Stove:
    • Place the stove on a flat, non-flammable surface away from overhanging branches.
    • Add small pieces of kindling to start, then gradually add larger pieces of wood.
    • Do not overfill the stove to ensure proper airflow.
    • Never leave the firepit unattended and make sure it is completely extinguished after use.
  • Please refer to the safety instructions provided with the stove for detailed guidance.
